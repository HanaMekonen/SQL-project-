SQL project on Covid 19 Data Exploration 

AS WHO defined Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus.The first known case was identified in Wuhan, China, in December 2019.
The disease quickly spread worldwide, resulting in the COVID-19 pandemic. This project present Covid 19 Data Exploration as global and africa level (and also it presents covid 19 data exploration of Etiopia and Eritrea).
The data taken from Our World in Data and built based on 207 country profiles.
The project basically adresses the following points:
  
 -Shows likelihood of dying if you contract covid (Total Cases vs Total Deaths)
 -Shows what percentage of population infected with Covid 19 
 -Compers African Countries with Highest Infection Rate compared to Population and with Highest Death Count per Population
 -Compers contintents with Highest Infection Rate compared to Population and with Highest Death Count per Population
 -Shows Percentage of Population that has recieved at least one Covid Vaccine (Total Population vs Vaccinations)
